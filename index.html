Why would I want to go spearfishing?

Spearfishing is a more active and adventurous branch of the great sport of fishing. It's a great way to get a swim in while trying to catch some tasty dinner. The active nature of spearfishing brings forth a great feeling of adrenaline and accomplishment that isn't always matched in traditional fishing. It allows you a glimpse of the underwater world as you become immersed to track down your prey.

What do I need to get started?

  • A speargun or polespear.

    You can find these online or in a dive shop.

  • A wet suit.

    Even if you're fishing in warmer water, prolonged exposure underwater can lead to unwanted results!

  • A boat

    Sometimes it may be necessary to navigate the waters to reach remote spots.

  • A diver down flag

    You'll want to let folks know you're diving in the area.
